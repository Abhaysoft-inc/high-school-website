IIT Delhi School

An exclusively English medium school, Lagna Devi Tarakant Senior Secondary School is setting new standard in the Education of children in 21st Century. It caters to the education of over 1200 students from LKG to 10+12, Governed by Lagna Devi Tarakant Shikshan Sansthan, the school is managed by a Management Committee.

The day to day functions are looked after by Principal/HOD. Lagna Devi Tarakant Senior Secondary School, Ranihawa is an institution founded in 2012, with a vision or spreading the value based education. The school strives to create facilities and infrastructure to provide students opportunities for learning, which will make them globally competitive.

It is a progressive English Medium Co-Educational Senior Secondary School, run by Lagna Devi Tarakant Shikshan Sansthan, Ranihawa, Deoria- 274 201 (U.P).
